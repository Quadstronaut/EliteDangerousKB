# Caustic Sink Launcher (Coriolis Tier-0)

AX caustic-venting utility. Ejects accumulated **caustic** damage the way a Heat Sink Launcher
ejects heat — firing a caustic sink immediately clears the caustic enzyme/cloud DoT that
Thargoid weapons and caustic environments apply. Essential survival kit vs Thargoid caustic
clouds, enzyme missiles, and Titan/Maelstrom approaches. **availability: live.**
