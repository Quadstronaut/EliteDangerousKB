# Seismic Charge Launcher (Coriolis module def)

Hardpoint mining tool for **deep core mining**. Plants timed charges in an asteroid's fissures;
detonating them at the correct combined yield cracks the rock open to expose the core deposits.
Group `scl`.

Key claims (from Coriolis JSON):
- **Class 2** only (Medium). Rating B. Damage 15, range 3000 m, mass 4 t, ammo 72, clip 1,
  power 1.2, thermal load 3.6.
- Fixed: cost 153,110 CR. Turret: cost 445,570 CR.
- Mounts: Fixed (F) and Turret (T). Requires a Class-2+ hardpoint.
