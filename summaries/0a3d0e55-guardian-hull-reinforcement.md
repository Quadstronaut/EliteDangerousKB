# Guardian Hull Reinforcement Package (ghrp) — Coriolis Tier-0

Powered optional internal. Classes 1–5, ratings **D & E only** (no C/B/A).
Requires the **Guardian Technology Broker** unlock (Guardian module).
ukDiscript: "Powered module that increases the ship's hull integrity and
resistance to Thargoid, caustic and thermic damage."

## Key claims
- **Powered** — unlike the standard Hull Reinforcement Package and Meta-Alloy
  HRP (both 0 power), Guardian HRP draws power (C1 D 0.56 MW → C5 D 1.46 MW).
- Resist profile: **causres 0.05 (5% caustic)**, **thermres 0.02 (2% thermal)**,
  explres 0, kinres 0. More caustic res than Meta-Alloy HRP (flat 3%) and adds a
  thermal slice; gives up conventional explosive/kinetic resists.
- **Highest raw hull HP of the three HRP lines.** C5: D 488 / E 450 vs standard
  HRP C5 D 390 / E 360 vs Meta-Alloy HRP C5 D 351 / E 324.
- Rating split (Coriolis symbol Class2 = D, Class1 = E): **D = more HP at half
  the mass for higher cost; E = cheaper, double mass, slightly less HP.**
