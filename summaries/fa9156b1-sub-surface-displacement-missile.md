# Sub-Surface Displacement Missile (Coriolis module def)

Hardpoint mining tool. Fires a penetrating missile that detonates **below** an asteroid's surface
to expel sub-surface deposits for Collector Limpets — the third extraction method alongside
laser (surface) and seismic (core). Group `sdm`.

  power 0.42. Fixed cost 12,600 CR / Turret 38,750 CR.
