# Type-8 Transporter

The Type-8 Transporter is a **medium-class dedicated hauler** by **Lakon Spaceways**,
introduced in 2024. It is the **strongest medium-pad cargo ship** — a class-7 plus three
class-6 optional internals let it out-haul a Python while still fitting an outpost's medium
pad. Unusually for a Lakon trader it carries a respectable six weapon mounts for self-defence.
It is stocked at [[locations/deciat]]'s Garay Terminal shipyard.

## Overview

- **Manufacturer:** Lakon Spaceways
- **Size class:** 2 (Medium landing pad — docks at outposts and all larger ports)
- **Role:** Medium-pad cargo hauler (bulk trade, mission running, colonisation supply)
- **Hull cost:** 34,820,576 CR (hull only)
- **Retail cost:** 38,453,970 CR (with stock modules)
- **Crew seats:** 1 (single pilot, no multicrew)

## Slot Layout

- **Core internals:** Power Plant **5**, Thrusters **5**, Frame Shift Drive **5**,
  Life Support **3**, Power Distributor **4**, Sensors **3**, Fuel Tank **5** (32 t).
- **Hardpoints:** 1 × Medium + 5 × Small (six weapon mounts — strong self-defence for a hauler).
- **Utility mounts:** 4.
- **Optional internals:** sizes 7, 6, 6, 6, 5, 5, 4, 2, 1 (nine slots) plus a reserved class-1
  **Planetary Approach Suite** (planet-landing and SRV capable). No cargo-restricted slots — all
  optionals are general-purpose.

The class-7 plus three class-6 internals make the Type-8 the **best medium-pad cargo hauler**,
carrying more than a Python Mk II while still fitting outposts. The size-5 FSD ceiling keeps
laden jump range modest — engineer the FSD at [[engineers/felicity-farseer]] (Increased Range)
and consider a Frame Shift Drive (SCO) for faster in-system transit.

## Acquisition

Sold at large stations carrying a shipyard. Confirmed in stock at **Garay Terminal**
(Coriolis Starport) in [[locations/deciat]], which also stocks Frame Shift Drives (standard
and SCO) and full core internals — buy the hull and engineer it at Farseer Inc in the same
system. Check Spansh (`spansh.co.uk/stations`) for nearest stock.
