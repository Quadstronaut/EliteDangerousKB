# Cobra Mk V

The Cobra Mk V is a **small-class multirole ship** by **Faulcon DeLacy**, a modern
successor to the classic [[ships/cobra-mk-iii|Cobra Mk III]] line, introduced in late
2024. It trades the Cobra Mk III's bare-bones agility for more internal capacity, four
utility mounts, and a
three-seat multicrew cockpit, while keeping a small (size-1) landing pad so it can
dock anywhere. It is stocked at [[locations/deciat]]'s Garay Terminal shipyard.

## Overview

- **Manufacturer:** Faulcon DeLacy
- **Size class:** 1 (Small landing pad — docks at outposts and planetary ports)
- **Role:** Multirole (combat, light hauling, planetary ops)
- **Hull cost:** 1,477,085 CR (hull only)
- **Retail cost:** 1,989,461 CR (with stock modules)
- **Crew seats:** 3 (multicrew capable)

## Slot Layout

- **Core internals:** Power Plant **4**, Thrusters **4**, Frame Shift Drive **4**,
  Life Support **3**, Power Distributor **4**, Sensors **3**, Fuel Tank **4**.
- **Hardpoints:** 3 × Medium + 2 × Small.
- **Utility mounts:** 4.
- **Optional internals:** sizes 5, 4, 4, 4, 3, 3, 3, 2, 1 (nine slots) plus a
  reserved class-1 **Planetary Approach Suite** (planet-landing and SRV capable).

The size-4 FSD ceiling means modest base jump range for its mass; pair it with
[[engineers/felicity-farseer]]'s Increased Range blueprint to make it a capable
small explorer. The 3M + 2S hardpoint count is strong firepower for a small hull.

## Acquisition

Sold at large stations carrying a shipyard. Confirmed in stock at **Garay Terminal**
(Coriolis Starport) in [[locations/deciat]], which also stocks Frame Shift Drives
(standard and SCO) and full core internals — buy the hull and engineer it at Farseer
Inc in the same system. Check Spansh (`spansh.co.uk/stations`) for nearest stock.
