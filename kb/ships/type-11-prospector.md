# Type-11 Prospector

The Type-11 Prospector is a **medium-class dedicated mining ship** by **Lakon Spaceways**,
the newest entry in the Lakon line. It is purpose-built for industrial mining: half of its
hardpoints are **mining-tool-only mounts**, it carries a dedicated limpet-controller bay and
a built-in fighter hangar, and a class-7 power distributor keeps a full rack of mining lasers,
abrasion blasters and seismic charges running at once. It fits a medium pad, so it can work
asteroid belts and dock at outposts a Type-9 cannot. It is stocked at [[locations/deciat]]'s
Garay Terminal shipyard.

## Overview

- **Manufacturer:** Lakon Spaceways
- **Size class:** 2 (Medium landing pad — docks at outposts and all larger ports)
- **Role:** Dedicated mining platform (core, laser, and sub-surface mining)
- **Hull cost:** 66,346,710 CR (hull only)
- **Retail cost:** 67,861,850 CR (with stock modules)
- **Crew seats:** 3 (multicrew capable) · built-in fighter hangar

## Slot Layout

- **Core internals:** Power Plant **6**, Thrusters **5**, Frame Shift Drive **5**,
  Life Support **3**, Power Distributor **7**, Sensors **3**, Fuel Tank **5**. The class-7
  power distributor is unusually large for the hull size — it sustains multiple mining tools
  firing continuously.
- **Hardpoints:** 8 weapon mounts. Four are **mining-tool-only** (sizes 3, 2, 2, 1 — accept
  mining lasers, abrasion blasters, sub-surface displacement missiles, seismic charge launchers
  and pulse wave analysers). Four are general weapon mounts (sizes 2, 1, 1, 1) for self-defence.
- **Utility mounts:** 4.
- **Optional internals:** sizes 6, 6, 6, 5, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1, 1 (ten general slots) plus a
  **class-5 limpet-controller-only** bay, a **class-5 fighter hangar** slot, and a reserved
  class-1 **Planetary Approach Suite**. The dedicated limpet bay frees general slots for
  refinery, cargo racks and a prospector/collector controller without sacrificing capacity.

A mining fit pairs the mining hardpoints with a Refinery, Detailed Surface Scanner, collector
and prospector limpet controllers, and cargo racks across the class-6 optionals. The class-5
FSD ceiling keeps jump range modest — engineer it at [[engineers/felicity-farseer]] (Increased
Range) for transit between mining sites.

## Acquisition

Sold at large stations carrying a shipyard. Confirmed in stock at **Garay Terminal**
(Coriolis Starport) in [[locations/deciat]], which also stocks Frame Shift Drives (standard
and SCO), refineries, limpet controllers and full core internals — buy the hull and engineer
it at Farseer Inc in the same system. Check Spansh (`spansh.co.uk/stations`) for nearest stock.
