# Mandalay

The Mandalay is a **medium-class long-range explorer** by **Zorgon Peterson**, introduced in
late 2024. It is one of the lightest medium hulls in the game (230 t) and pairs that low mass
with a class-5 Frame Shift Drive and a class-6 optional bay for a large fuel scoop, giving it
class-leading jump range for explorers and Trailblazers prospecting runs. It is also strikingly
agile for an explorer, with a roll rate close to a dedicated combat ship. Its classic, cheaper
predecessor in the explorer role is the [[ships/asp-explorer]] — heavier and a little
shorter-ranged, but far less costly and with deep internal room. The budget, small-pad member of
the explorer line is the cold-running [[ships/diamondback-explorer]].

## Overview

- **Manufacturer:** Zorgon Peterson
- **Size class:** 2 (Medium landing pad — docks at outposts and all larger ports)
- **Role:** Long-range exploration (also light multirole / passenger)
- **Hull cost:** 16,527,192 CR (hull only)
- **Retail cost:** 17,639,220 CR (with stock modules)
- **Crew seats:** 2 (multicrew capable)

## Slot Layout

- **Core internals:** Power Plant **5**, Thrusters **5**, Frame Shift Drive **5**,
  Life Support **4**, Power Distributor **5**, Sensors **5**, Fuel Tank **5**. The class-5 FSD
  on a 230 t hull is what drives the Mandalay's long jump range.
- **Hardpoints:** 4 × Medium + 2 × Small (six weapon mounts — strong for an explorer that may
  need to fight its way out of trouble).
- **Utility mounts:** 4 (room for a Detailed Surface Scanner support kit, heat sinks, and
  shield boosters).
- **Optional internals:** sizes 6, 5, 4, 4, 3, 3, 2, 1, 1, 1 (ten slots) plus a reserved class-1
  **Planetary Approach Suite** (planet-landing and SRV capable). The class-6 slot takes a large
  fuel scoop or Guardian FSD Booster; remaining slots cover an AFMU, SRV hangar, shields and a
  Detailed Surface Scanner.

A standard exploration build fits an engineered class-5 FSD, a class-6 fuel scoop, an AFMU,
an SRV hangar and a Detailed Surface Scanner. Engineer the FSD at [[engineers/felicity-farseer]]
(Increased Range) and add a Guardian FSD Booster to push the jump range toward the top of the
explorer class.

## Acquisition

Sold at large stations carrying a shipyard. As a recent Zorgon Peterson hull it is widely
stocked at major shipyards — check Spansh (`spansh.co.uk/stations`) for the nearest source,
then engineer the FSD at Farseer Inc in [[locations/deciat]].
