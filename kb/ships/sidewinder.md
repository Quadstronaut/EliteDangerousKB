# Sidewinder

The Sidewinder Mk I is **Faulcon DeLacy's tiny size-1 multirole** — the universal free starter ship.
Every new commander begins in one, and it is the ship insurance defaults you to if you cannot afford
the rebuy on anything else. It is the **cheapest hull paged in this KB** and its **second-lightest** — behind only the
14 t [[ships/hauler]]: a 25 t, 4,588 CR baseline against which every other ship is measured. Fragile, shallow-cored and lightly
armed, it is nonetheless nimble and cheap enough to never truly lose — the floor of the entire
ship roster.

## Overview

- **Manufacturer:** Faulcon DeLacy
- **Size class:** 1 (Small landing pad — docks anywhere, including outposts and planetary ports)
- **Role:** Starter multirole (the default free ship; basic combat, courier, short hops)
- **Rank requirement:** none — credits only (no `requirements` block in the Coriolis data)
- **Hull cost:** 4,588 CR (hull only — the cheapest hull in the KB)
- **Retail cost:** 32,000 CR (with stock modules)
- **Crew seats:** 1

## Slot Layout

- **Core internals:** Power Plant **2**, Thrusters **2**, Frame Shift Drive **2**,
  Life Support **1**, Power Distributor **1**, Sensors **1**, Fuel Tank **1**. A minimal core
  capped at class-2 — the shallowest core in the KB, and the reason the Sidewinder is the baseline
  every other ship improves on.
- **Hardpoints:** 2 × Small (two weapon mounts) — the smallest armament of any ship paged here.
- **Utility mounts:** 2 (room for a chaff launcher, heat sink, or a small shield booster).
- **Optional internals:** sizes 2, 2, 1, 1, 1, 1 (six slots, top class-2) plus a reserved class-1
  **Planetary Approach Suite**. **No Military slot.**
- **Military slots:** none.

Bulkheads carry `causres 0` on every grade.

## The baseline ship and first upgrades

- **vs [[ships/viper-mk-iii]]:** the Viper Mk III is the natural first dedicated combat step up —
  far faster (320/400 vs 220/320), with four mounts (2 Medium + 2 Small) and a class-3 Military
  slot, for ~97 k CR. The Sidewinder's two Small mounts and class-2 core are the entry rung beneath
  it.
- **vs [[ships/cobra-mk-iii]]:** the Cobra Mk III is the classic multirole upgrade — eight
  optionals, four mounts and far more cargo/role flexibility while staying small-pad and cheap. It
  is the ship many commanders graduate to once the Sidewinder's six shallow optionals run out.
- **As the free default:** because it is the cheapest hull in the KB, the Sidewinder is what you
  fall back to on insurance default and a zero-risk platform for learning flight, docking and basic
  combat before committing credits to a larger hull.

## Build notes

With a class-2 core and two Small hardpoints there is little to optimise — the Sidewinder is a
learning and fallback platform, not a build target. A pair of gimballed multi-cannons or pulse
lasers, a small shield, and a chaff or heat sink in the utility mounts covers basic self-defence.
Spend credits on the next hull rather than engineering this one; its value is that it is effectively
free to replace.

## Acquisition

Issued free to every new commander and stocked at essentially every shipyard. It is also the ship
insurance assigns when you cannot cover a rebuy, so you are never permanently grounded.
