# Panther Clipper Mk II

The Panther Clipper Mk II is a **large-class dedicated hauler** by **Zorgon Peterson**,
introduced in 2025. It is the **largest-cargo ship in the game** — two unrestricted class-8
optional internals plus two class-7 slots let it haul more than any other hull. Despite its
freighter role it carries a heavy weapon and utility loadout for self-defence and a four-seat
multicrew cockpit, and it can mount a Ship-Launched Fighter bay. It needs a **Large landing
pad** and is stocked at [[locations/deciat]]'s Garay Terminal shipyard.

## Overview

- **Manufacturer:** Zorgon Peterson
- **Size class:** 3 (Large landing pad — large orbital and planetary ports only)
- **Role:** Heavy cargo hauler (max-tonnage trade, fleet logistics, colonisation supply)
- **Hull cost:** 286,905,678 CR (hull only)
- **Retail cost:** 301,348,586 CR (with stock modules)
- **Crew seats:** 4 (multicrew capable)
- **Fighter hangar:** yes (can equip a Ship-Launched Fighter bay)

## Slot Layout

- **Core internals:** Power Plant **8**, Thrusters **8**, Frame Shift Drive **7**,
  Life Support **5**, Power Distributor **7**, Sensors **5**, Fuel Tank **7** (128 t).
- **Hardpoints:** 2 × Large + 4 × Medium + 4 × Small (ten weapon mounts).
- **Utility mounts:** 6.
- **Optional internals:** sizes 8, 8, 7, 7, 6, 6, 6, 5, 5, 4, 2, 1 (twelve slots) plus a
  reserved class-1 **Planetary Approach Suite** (planet-landing and SRV capable). The first
  size-8 and first size-7 slots are **cargo/fuel-restricted** (cargo rack or fuel tank only).

The twin class-8 internals (256 t cargo rack each) plus twin class-7 slots give the Panther
Clipper Mk II the **largest cargo capacity of any ship**. The size-7 FSD ceiling on a 1200 t
hull means a short laden jump range — pair it with [[engineers/felicity-farseer]]'s Increased
Range blueprint and a Frame Shift Drive (SCO) for tolerable transit. Its ten hardpoints and
six utility mounts make it far better armed than older haulers like the Type-9.

## Acquisition

Sold at large stations carrying a shipyard. Confirmed in stock at **Garay Terminal**
(Coriolis Starport) in [[locations/deciat]], which also stocks Frame Shift Drives (standard
and SCO) and full core internals — buy the hull and engineer it at Farseer Inc in the same
system. Check Spansh (`spansh.co.uk/stations`) for nearest stock.
