# Adder

The Adder is **Zorgon Peterson's cheap small-pad multipurpose** — the budget early-game
explorer/hauler that rounds out the starter tier alongside the fighter line ([[ships/eagle]] /
[[ships/viper-mk-iii]]). Where those ships turn and fight, the Adder is the cheap utility
alternative: a light 35 t hull with a class-3 FSD and a class-3 fuel tank give it a surprisingly long
jump range for its size and price, and a Medium hardpoint plus seven optional internals make it a
credible budget trader, miner or first explorer.

## Overview

- **Manufacturer:** Zorgon Peterson
- **Size class:** 1 (Small landing pad — docks anywhere, including outposts and planetary ports)
- **Role:** Budget multipurpose (early-game exploration, light hauling, courier, mining)
- **Rank requirement:** none — credits only (no `requirements` block in the Coriolis data)
- **Hull cost:** 40,833 CR (hull only — the fourth-cheapest hull in the KB, above the Sidewinder, Eagle and Hauler)
- **Retail cost:** 87,808 CR (with stock modules)
- **Crew seats:** 2

## Slot Layout

- **Core internals:** Power Plant **3**, Thrusters **3**, Frame Shift Drive **3**, Life Support **1**,
  Power Distributor **2**, Sensors **3**, Fuel Tank **3**. The class-3 FSD paired with a class-3 fuel
  tank on a 35 t hull is the data behind the Adder's reputation for good jump range — long legs for so
  cheap and small a ship.
- **Hardpoints:** 1 × Medium + 2 × Small (three weapon mounts) — one more, and one larger, than the
  Sidewinder's two Small.
- **Utility mounts:** 2 (shield booster, chaff, heat sink, or point defence).
- **Optional internals:** sizes 3, 3, 2, 2, 1, 1, 1 (seven regular slots, top class-3, two class-3)
  plus a reserved class-1 **Planetary Approach Suite**. **No Military slot.**
- **Military slots:** none.

Bulkheads carry `causres 0` on every grade.

## The budget utility alternative

- **vs [[ships/sidewinder]]:** the Adder is the cheap step up in capability from the free starter —
  seven optionals (vs six), a Medium hardpoint, an extra crew seat and a much better range package
  (class-3 FSD + class-3 fuel tank), for ~88 k CR. The trade is a heavier hull and a slightly lower
  roll.
- **vs [[ships/eagle]] / [[ships/viper-mk-iii]]:** those are dedicated small-pad fighters (agility and
  weapon mounts); the Adder is the utility counterpart — fewer combat credentials but deeper internals,
  longer legs and more cargo/role flexibility. Pick the Adder when the early-game goal is exploration,
  hauling or mining rather than dogfighting.
- **vs [[ships/cobra-mk-iii]]:** the Cobra Mk III is the classic multirole step up — eight optionals,
  four mounts (2 Medium + 2 Small), and far more cargo/jump capacity while staying small-pad. The Adder
  is the cheaper, lighter rung beneath it for commanders not yet ready to spend on the Cobra.

## Build notes

The Adder rewards a focused fit. For exploration, lean on the low mass: an A-rated class-3 FSD, a fuel
scoop, a Detailed Surface Scanner and an Advanced Discovery Scanner turn it into a cheap first deep-space
ship with respectable range. For hauling, fill the seven optionals with cargo racks and a basic shield.
The single Medium hardpoint plus two Small mounts cover self-defence but the Adder is not a combat hull —
its strength is doing a job cheaply. With no Military slot, hull reinforcement competes with cargo in the
optional slots, so keep the role single-purpose.

## Acquisition

Stocked at most shipyards; no rank or permit requirement, credits only. Its low price and small pad make
it an accessible early purchase for commanders moving beyond the Sidewinder.
