# HIP 23759

HIP 23759 is an Independent system on the edge of the **Witch Head Nebula**, known in the
KB as a **Meta-Alloys source** used to unlock engineer [[engineers/felicity-farseer]]. Its
scoopable blue-white primary makes it an easy fuel stop on the run out to the nebula.

## System Overview

- **Allegiance:** Independent
- **Government:** Corporate
- **Controlling faction:** Xeno Research Group
- **Security:** Medium
- **Economy:** Service (secondary: Agriculture)
- **Reserve level:** Pristine
- **Population:** ~121,000

> Corroboration: the controlling faction (**Xeno Research Group**) and the **Service** primary /
> **Agriculture** secondary economy are confirmed independently by the EDSM stations endpoint —
> Xeno Research Group controls the asteroid base and most settlements, and Agriculture is the
> dominant settlement economy.

## Stations & Services

EDSM lists **87 stations** in HIP 23759, the large majority being **Fleet Carriers** (transient,
ignore for planning) and **Odyssey Settlements** (planetary, Agriculture / Tourism / Extraction).
The permanent, non-carrier infrastructure is:

| Station | Type | Economy | Market | Outfitting | Faction | Dist |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Witch Head Science Centre** | Asteroid base | Service | Yes | No | Xeno Research Group | ~2,423 ls |
| **Columbi's Haven** | Outpost | Tourism | No | No | The Allied Commission | ~2,423 ls |

- The **Witch Head Science Centre** asteroid base is the system's only permanent commodity
  market (large pad). It is the station relevant to Meta-Alloy buying (see below).
- **No "Coles Point" station exists** in HIP 23759 — earlier notes naming it were mistaken.
- Settlement factions span **Xeno Research Group** (Independent/Corporate, dominant),
  **The Allied Commission** (Alliance/Corporate), and **Witch Head Troop**
  (Independent/Dictatorship).

## KB Relevance — Felicity Farseer unlock

HIP 23759 supplies **Meta-Alloys** on the commodity market at the **Witch Head Science Centre**
asteroid base. Delivering 1 unit of Meta-Alloys to Farseer Inc. is the second unlock requirement
for [[engineers/felicity-farseer]]; Meta-Alloys can also be harvested from Thargoid Barnacle
sites (see Canonn's barnacle map).

- **Direct market evidence (EDSM, 2026-06-15):** the Witch Head Science Centre market lists
  **Meta-Alloys** at `buyPrice ≈ 148,408 cr`. At capture the listing showed **stock 0**
  (`stockBracket 0`) — the commodity is a standing market entry but supply is BGS-driven.

> market listing (not just the Farseer page), so `verified: true`; live *stock*, however, is
> never guaranteed.
