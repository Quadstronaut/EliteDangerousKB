See [[ships/viper-mk-iv]] for the Viper Mk IV (Faulcon DeLacy tankier small-pad multirole; the Mk III's heavier sibling — 190 t, double the shield/armour (150/150), bigger core with a class-4 fuel tank and eight optionals, but slower (270/340), same 2 Medium + 2 Small hardpoints + one class-3 Military slot, no rank gate).
See [[ships/sidewinder]] for the Sidewinder (Faulcon DeLacy free starter; the cheapest hull in the KB (4,588 CR) and its 2nd-lightest (25 t, behind the [[ships/hauler]]'s 14 t), class-2 core, 2 Small hardpoints, roll 110 (ties the Vulture, now 2nd to the Eagle Mk II’s 120), no Military slot, no rank gate — the universal baseline ship).
See [[ships/eagle]] for the Eagle Mk II (Core Dynamics cheap small-pad fighter; the NEW nimblest roll in the KB (120, above the prior Sidewinder/Vulture 110) and highest pitch, 3 Small hardpoints + 1 utility, one class-2 Military slot, lowest base armour (40), 2nd-cheapest hull, no rank gate — the rookie’s affordable dogfighter).
See [[ships/adder]] for the Adder (Zorgon Peterson cheap small-pad multipurpose; 3rd-lightest hull (35 t), class-3 FSD + class-3 fuel tank for budget jump range, 1 Medium + 2 Small hardpoints, seven optionals, no Military slot, no rank gate — the budget early-game explorer/hauler).
See [[ships/imperial-eagle]] for the Imperial Eagle (Gutamaya's Imperial refinement of the Eagle Mk II; faster (300/400) and better-shielded (80 MJ) with a Medium hardpoint + class-3 power plant and one class-2 Military slot, but roll 100 (short of the base Eagle's class-leading 120), no rank gate — the upgraded small-pad skirmisher).
See [[ships/hauler]] for the Hauler (Zorgon Peterson tiny dedicated freighter; the NEW lightest hull in the KB (14 t) and lowest heat capacity (123), class-2 core, 1 Small hardpoint + 2 utility, six optionals (top two class-3), no Military slot, no rank gate — the cheapest cargo run / lightest budget jumper).
See [[ships/imperial-courier]] for the Imperial Courier (Gutamaya's fast small-pad shield-brawler; three Medium hardpoints (the most Medium mounts of any KB small-pad hull) + 4 utility, 200 MJ shield (2nd-highest small-pad shield, behind the Vulture), bigger class-4 PP / class-3 PD core, 35 t (ties the Adder for joint 3rd-lightest), thin armour, no Military slot — and, unlike the Imperial Eagle, **rank-gated** (Empire Master)).
See [[ships/dolphin]] for the Dolphin (Saud Kruger small-pad luxury passenger liner; the first Saud Kruger hull in the KB and the entry rung of the Dolphin → Orca → Beluga line — Luxury-class cabin capable, nine optional internals to fill with cabins, class-5 thrusters + class-4 tank for good pace/range on a light 140 t hull, just 2 Small hardpoints, no Military slot, no rank gate — the classic first passenger ship).
See [[ships/type-6-transporter]] for the Type-6 Transporter (Lakon cheap medium-pad dedicated freighter; the cheapest medium-pad hull in the KB (866 k CR), class-4 FSD + class-4 fuel tank for budget jump range, deep optionals (top two class-5), just 2 Small hardpoints, no Military slot, no rank gate — the classic "first real trader", the medium-pad rung up from the Hauler).
See [[ships/keelback]] for the Keelback (Lakon's armed, fighter-capable variant of the Type-6 airframe; 2 Medium + 2 Small hardpoints, a Ship-Launched Fighter bay, ~double the Type-6's shield/armour, no rank gate — the **cheapest fighter-bay-capable hull in the KB** (~3 M CR) and the second-cheapest medium-pad hull, behind only its Type-6 sibling).
See [[ships/alliance-crusader]] for the Alliance Crusader (medium-pad AX multicrew gunship; only Alliance medium with a fighter bay, 3 Military slots, crew 4).
See [[ships/federal-corvette]] for the Federal Corvette (large-pad combat flagship; first KB ship with Huge hardpoints (two), class-8 PP/PD, Rear Admiral rank gate).
See [[ships/federal-dropship]] for the Federal Dropship (Core Dynamics base medium combat/troop gunship; the first rung of the Federal medium line, 580 t tanky brawler, 1 Large + 4 Medium hardpoints + two class-4 Military slots, no fighter bay, Federal Navy **Midshipman** rank gate — the medium-pad Federal counterpart to the Alliance AX trio).
See [[ships/federal-assault-ship]] for the Federal Assault Ship (Core Dynamics speed/agility variant — the middle hull of the Federal medium trio; the lightest at 480 t (its siblings are 580 t), fastest (210/350) and most agile, the only Federal medium with **2 Large** hardpoints (2 Large + 2 Medium = 4 mounts, fewest of the trio but biggest-calibre), two class-4 Military slots, no fighter bay, Federal Navy **Chief Petty Officer** rank gate — between the Dropship's Midshipman and the Gunship's Ensign).
See [[ships/federal-gunship]] for the Federal Gunship (Core Dynamics heavy fighter-bay medium gunship; the Dropship airframe up-armed — 1 Large + 4 Medium + 2 Small (seven mounts, most of the Federal mediums) + three class-4 Military slots + class-7 PD + a Ship-Launched Fighter bay, but slowest (170/280) and shallowest internals (six optionals), Federal Navy **Ensign** rank gate).
See [[ships/anaconda]] for the Anaconda (large-pad no-gate jack-of-all-trades; one Huge but eight total mounts, deepest optionals, best jump range, no rank gate).
See [[ships/imperial-cutter]] for the Imperial Cutter (large-pad shield-tank/trader; highest base shield (600 MJ), two class-8 optionals, least agile, Duke rank gate).
See [[ships/imperial-clipper]] for the Imperial Clipper (Gutamaya's fast large-pad multirole; **the fastest large-pad ship in the KB** (300/380), 2 Large + 2 Medium hardpoints (no Small), light 400 t hull, nine optionals (top class-7), no Military slot, Empire **Baron** rank gate — the speed alternative to the Cutter, and the large-pad step up the Imperial line beyond the small-pad Eagle/Courier pair).
See [[ships/type-10-defender]] for the Type-10 Defender (large-pad AX armour brick; Lakon, highest base armour (580) & hardness (75) in the KB, nine weapon mounts but no Huge, two Military slots, no rank gate).

The Trailblazers colonisation programme (Feb 2025) allows commanders and squadrons to claim
and develop unclaimed systems using fleet carriers and construction commodities.

## AX / Thargoid Content

The Thargoid war narrative concluded, but the content remains fully live: Spire sites,
Titan wrecks, AX combat zones, and Thargoid interceptor encounters all remain accessible.
These are tagged `availability: live` throughout the KB.
AX weapons — **kinetic**: [[outfitting/ax-multi-cannon]] (standard workhorse, no unlock) and its
[[outfitting/ax-multi-cannon-enhanced|Enhanced]] gimballed upgrade; **explosive**:
[[outfitting/ax-missile-rack]] (dumbfire AX missiles) plus its
[[outfitting/ax-missile-rack-enhanced|Enhanced]] upgrade, the caustic
[[outfitting/enzyme-missile-rack|Enzyme Missile Rack]] (hull damage-over-time) and the seeking
[[outfitting/nanite-torpedo-pylon|Nanite Torpedo Pylon]] (nanite vent-disruption payload); the
**Guardian trio** [[outfitting/guardian-gauss-cannon]] (long-range thermal),
[[outfitting/guardian-plasma-charger]] (absolute-damage charge burst) and
[[outfitting/guardian-shard-cannon]] (close-range thermal shotgun);
plus **anti-swarm support** [[outfitting/remote-release-flak-launcher]] (explosive flak) and
[[outfitting/remote-release-flechette-launcher]] (kinetic flechette) vs Swarm/Scout clouds.
AX defence — the Guardian defensive trio [[outfitting/guardian-shield-reinforcement]],
[[outfitting/hull-reinforcement|Guardian HRP]] and [[outfitting/module-reinforcement|Guardian MRP]].
AX hulls — the medium-pad platforms this kit goes on: the [[ships/alliance-chieftain]]
(agile, high-hardness brawler with three dedicated Military reinforcement slots) and its tankier
sibling the [[ships/alliance-challenger]] (more hull/shield + a deeper optional bank, also 3
Military slots, but slower); the multicrew [[ships/alliance-crusader]] (the trio's only fighter-bay
hull, crew 4, also 3 Military slots); plus the higher-firepower [[ships/krait-mk-ii]] (3 large
hardpoints + a Ship-Launched Fighter bay) and its lighter long-range explorer sibling the
[[ships/krait-phantom]] (no fighter bay, no Military slots). For a **large-pad** step up, the
**large-pad combat trinity** plus the dedicated armour brick: the [[ships/federal-corvette]] (Core
Dynamics, two Huge hardpoints + class-8 PP/PD — the highest-firepower AX gunship, Rear Admiral rank
gate), the [[ships/anaconda]] (Faulcon DeLacy, one Huge but eight total mounts + deepest optionals +
best jump range — and **no rank gate**), the [[ships/imperial-cutter]] (Gutamaya, the shield-tank —
highest base shield 600 MJ + two class-8 optionals, Duke rank gate) and the [[ships/type-10-defender]]
(Lakon, the armour brick — highest armour 580 + hardness 75 + nine weapon mounts but no Huge, the
heaviest hull, **no rank gate**).
AX utilities — the survival/targeting trio every Thargoid-capable ship fits:
[[outfitting/xeno-scanner]] (IDs the Thargoid and reveals its weak points),
[[outfitting/shutdown-field-neutraliser]] (negates the EMP shutdown pulse) and
[[outfitting/caustic-sink-launcher]] (purges caustic DoT, the heat-sink analogue);
plus the optional-internal [[outfitting/decontamination-limpet-controller]] (a limpet that
removes caustic contamination over time + minor hull repair — self or ally).
