# Felicity Farseer

Felicity Farseer is the first exploration engineer most commanders unlock. Located in the
[[locations/deciat]] system (Farseer Inc. surface port on Deciat 6a).

## Unlock Requirements

To receive an invitation from Felicity Farseer:

1. Achieve an exploration rank of **Scout** or higher (second rank in the Exploration line).
2. Deliver **1 unit of Meta-Alloys** to Farseer Inc. in Deciat.

Meta-Alloys **cannot be bought at Farseer Inc itself** — its market lists Meta-Alloys at
`demand 0` / `stock 0` (EDSM, 2026-06-15), so you must bring them in. Source them from the
**Witch Head Science Centre** asteroid base in [[locations/hip-23759]] (Witch Head Nebula),
which carries Meta-Alloys on its commodity market, or harvest them from Thargoid Barnacle sites
found via Canonn's barnacle map.

## Offered Blueprints

Felicity Farseer offers blueprints for:

- **Frame Shift Drive** (grades 1–5): Increased FSD Range, Faster Boot Sequence
- **Thrusters** (grades 1–3): Dirty Drives, Clean Drives
- **Sensors** (grades 1–3): Lightweight Sensors, Long Range Sensors

## Notes

Felicity Farseer is located at a **planetary surface port** — you need a ship with a
Planetary Approach Suite (standard on all ships except fighters) and SRV Hangar to land.
No SRV is required just to dock and trade, but the port is on a 0.21g moon.

Farseer Inc.'s market is **sell-only** (no commodity supply) — do not plan to restock there.
